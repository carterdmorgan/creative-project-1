About John Donovan

John Donovan is a lifelong native of Washington D.C. Born in 1982, he grew up enjoying all of the beautiful sights of our nation's capitol. His love of his nation inspired him to share its beauty with all he could.

John first began his pursuit of photography at the age of 11, when he bought a disposable camera to take a picture of a stack of waffles. That stack of waffles grew into a mountain of dreams, as John's skills increased each day. Eventually, he began photographing our nation's beautiful monuments, hoping to inspire others with his pictures.

John has a lovely wife and a pet dog. Outside of photography, John loves skateboarding, video games, and eating pizza.
